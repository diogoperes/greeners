Smart Sprinklers

The sprinklers will make use of soil moisture sensors to help manage the irrigation system and check if it's required for the sprinklers to start working.

This system will be connected to the central hub that will get the weather forecast to check the best time of the day to irrigate and to prevent irrigating before raining.

Smart Bulbs

The smart bulbs will have brightness sensors to detect the surrounding luminosity and adjust the bulb's brightness to save energy.

Each bulb will also have the possibility to work with motion detection and to configure working cycles.

Air Conditioner System

Did you know you can save up to nine percent for every degree you raise the thermostat during hot months? The air conditioner system will automatically increase temperature settings when no one's at home or at night while sleeping so that cooling is not wasted.

Temperature and humidity sensors placed on the building's interior and exterior will maximize the air conditioner system functioning and give you advices on how to save energy.

If you have electric blinds, it will be possible to connect them to the system to automatically close them when no one's at home and increase the air conditioner efficiency before you arrive.
