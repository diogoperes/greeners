About Us

We are a team of two computer engineers and a QA engineer from Connect Enterprises , based on Lisbon, Portugal. We like challenges, so we accepted a challenged created by our company called Change Box, a variant of Adobe's Kickbox project, focused on the theme: hep combat climate change. Our idea, that we are presenting to you now, was one of the top three ideas, so we are doing a market research on our concept to analyse its potential.

Our mission

Greeners mission is to help combat energy and water waste, by automating and increasing the efficiency of multiple devices used on infrastructures all around the world.
